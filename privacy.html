Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

Zzzap ("we", "our", "the app") is a polling app that helps you make quick group decisions. We take your privacy seriously and collect only what we need to make the app work.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address and display name.
  • Poll data: Questions, answer options, votes, and recipient lists you create are stored on our servers (Firebase) to deliver the polling experience.
  • Push notification tokens: We store your device token to send you notifications about polls you've received or created.

Voice Zzzaps

When you use voice zzzaps, your speech is transcribed on your device using Apple's speech recognition. The transcribed text may be sent to our servers to parse your zzzap. No audio is ever uploaded and transcripts are not stored after parsing.

Voice Improvement Data (Opt-In)

You can choose to help improve voice zzzap accuracy by opting in via Profile → Settings → "Help Improve Zzzap." If you opt in, we collect anonymous data including the AI-parsed result and whether you edited it before sending. This data contains no audio recordings, no personal information, and no content that identifies you or your recipients. It is stored anonymously and used solely to improve parsing accuracy.

You can opt out at any time in your Profile settings. Once you opt out, no further data is collected. Previously collected anonymous data may still be used for improvement purposes.

Contacts

If you use Find Friends, your contacts are matched against existing Zzzap users to help you connect. Contact data is hashed for matching and is not stored on our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not store audio recordings.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers in the United States. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS) and Firebase Security Rules to protect your data.

Data Retention

Poll data is retained for up to 30 days after a poll closes. You can delete your account at any time, which removes all associated data from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Zzzap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by updating this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@zzzap-app.com.
